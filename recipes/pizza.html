Pizza

Making a delicious homemade pizza from scratch involves preparing the dough, a simple sauce, adding your favorite toppings, and baking it to golden, bubbly perfection. The key to a great crust is a slow fermentation of the dough, which develops a more complex flavor and better texture. While pre-made bases are available, making your own ensures a fresh, authentic taste.

Ingredients

    Dough Ingredients:

  • 1 package active dry yeast (2 1/4 tsp)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3/4 cup warm water (105째F-115째F)
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil

    • Sauce Ingredients:

  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp Italian herbs (oregano, basil)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Toppings:

  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of vegetables (onions, bell peppers, mushrooms, olives) or protein (pepperoni, cooked sausage)
  • Cornmeal for dusting the pan

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a small bowl, stir together the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes foamy. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the foamy yeast mixture and olive oil, then mix and knead until a smooth, soft, slightly tacky dough forms.
  2. Let it Rise: Brush a large bowl with olive oil, place the dough inside, and turn to coat. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap or a damp cloth and set it aside in a warm place until the dough has doubled in size (about 1 to 1.5 hours).
  3. Prepare Oven and Toppings: Preheat your oven to its highest setting (around 475째F or 245째C) with a pizza stone or heavy baking sheet inside for at least 30 minutes. Prepare all your toppings and bring them to room temperature to prevent a soggy pizza.
  4. Assemble the Pizza: Punch down the dough and turn it out onto a lightly floured surface. Stretch or roll it into a round shape, leaving a slightly thicker edge for the crust. Transfer the base to a pizza peel or baking sheet dusted with cornmeal. Spread sauce evenly over the base, add cheese, and then your desired toppings.
  5. Bake: Slide the pizza into the preheated oven and bake for 10 to 15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
